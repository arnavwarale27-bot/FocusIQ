SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

One Webcam. Complete
Attention Intelligence.

FocusIQ combines 6 AI signals into a single live 0–100 Focus Score

👁️

Gaze Direction

Detects when you look away using MediaPipe 468-point face mesh telemetry.

😴

Drowsiness (EAR)

Eye Aspect Ratio detects micro-closure and fatigue trends in real time.

📐

Head Pose

Yaw/Pitch/Roll tracking via cv2.solvePnP knows exactly where you are looking.

🧍

Posture

Shoulder-ear angle alignment detects tech-neck and physiological slouching.

😤

Frustration

Rapid head movements and blink velocity spikes signal cognitive friction levels.

🎯

Focus Score

All signals fused into a smooth 0–100 score updated every single second.
